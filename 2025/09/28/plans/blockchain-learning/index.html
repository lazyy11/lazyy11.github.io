📅 8-Week Compressed Bitcoin & Blockchain Study Plan

3-step book/tutorial roadmap (foundations → technical → economics) with the 2-course video plan into one compressed 8-week plan. This way, > you’ll cover:

  • Books/Tutorials → Build intuition + technical + economic foundations.
  • Video Courses → Deepen understanding + get hands-on blockchain dev skills.

Weeks 1–2 → Step 1: Foundations (Conceptual + Gentle Intro)

Read:

  • 📖 The Basics of Bitcoins and Blockchains by Antony Lewis
  • 🌐 Learn Me A Bitcoin (skim for clarity on transactions, blocks, mining)

Video (Optional support):

  • Edureka Blockchain Full Course (YouTube, 8 hrs) → watch selected sections for an overview.

Goal: Build solid intuition about Bitcoin, wallets, mining, and why blockchains matter.

Weeks 3–4 → Step 2: Technical Deep Dive (Bitcoin Core)

Read:

  • 📖 Mastering Bitcoin by Andreas M. Antonopoulos (focus on Ch.1–6 for now)
  • 📚 Supplement: Mastering Blockchain by Imran Bashir (skim consensus algorithms: PoW, PoS, PBFT)

Video Course:

  • Bitcoin and Cryptocurrency Technologies (Princeton / Coursera)

    • Week 1: Crypto & transactions
    • Week 2: Bitcoin internals
    • Week 3: Mining & consensus
    • Week 4: Network, privacy, regulation

Goal: Deep understanding of how Bitcoin works under the hood (transactions, blocks, mining, consensus, network).

Weeks 5–6 → Step 3: Economics / Why It Matters

Read:

  • 📖 The Bitcoin Standard by Saifedean Ammous

Video (Optional lecture support):

  • MIT OCW – Blockchain and Money (Gary Gensler) → skim 2–3 lectures.

Goal: Understand Bitcoin’s economic significance: sound money, digital gold, and why decentralization matters.

Weeks 7–8 → Hands-On Blockchain Development

Video Course:

  • Patrick Collins – Learn Blockchain, Solidity, and Full Stack Web3 Development (YouTube, Free)

Focus Modules:

  • Week 7: Smart contracts, Solidity basics, deploy your first contract.
  • Week 8: DApps, ERC-20 tokens, oracles, connecting contracts to frontend.

Goal: Gain practical blockchain development skills — deploy contracts, build a simple dApp, and connect backend/frontend.

🎯 Final Outcome (After 8 Weeks)

  • Strong conceptual understanding of Bitcoin & blockchains.
  • Solid technical knowledge (protocols, consensus, cryptography).
  • Awareness of economic/monetary context (why Bitcoin matters).
  • Hands-on developer experience with smart contracts & dApps.
