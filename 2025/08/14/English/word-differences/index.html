Summary of Word Differences

词汇组 词义区分 例句
hallway, corridor 均指走廊，但 hallway 更常用于家庭或小建筑，corridor 更正式，多用于大型建筑（如医院、学校）。 - She left her shoes in the hallway.
- The hospital corridor was brightly lit.
stove, furnace, oven, fireplace - stove：烹饪用的炉具（可燃气/电）。
- furnace：供暖的大型设备（如锅炉）。
- oven：封闭的烘烤设备（内置于stove或独立）。
- fireplace：壁炉（装饰或取暖）。		 - She cooked soup on the gas stove.
- The furnace broke down in winter.
- Bake the cake in the oven at 180°C.
- They gathered around the fireplace.
terrace, porch - terrace：露台（户外平台，常与房屋相连或屋顶）。
- porch：门廊（房屋入口处有顶的延伸区域）。		 - They had dinner on the rooftop terrace.
- The rocking chair was placed on the front porch.
pamphlet, booklet, brochure, leaflet, flyer - pamphlet/booklet：小册子（booklet可能有装订）。
- brochure：彩印宣传册（如旅游景点）。
- leaflet：单页折页。
- flyer：单张广告传单。		 - The NGO distributed pamphlets about health.
- The hotel brochure featured pool photos.
- He handed out leaflets at the mall.
- A flyer advertised a concert.
overpass, viaduct - overpass：跨越另一条道路/铁路的公路立交桥。
- viaduct：由多跨组成的长高架桥（通常承载道路/铁路，跨越山谷/城区）。		 - Traffic flowed smoothly on the new overpass.
- The railway viaduct spans the valley.
carrot, turnip, radish - carrot：橙色胡萝卜。
- turnip：白/紫色芜菁（根茎类）。
- radish：红色/白色小萝卜（脆辣）。		 - Carrots are rich in vitamin A.
- Turnips are often used in stews.
- She added sliced radish to the salad.
internship, practicum, apprenticeship, probation, fieldwork - internship：实习（学生/职场）。
- practicum：实践课程（如教育/医疗）。
- apprenticeship：学徒制（手工艺/技术）。
- probation：试用期或缓刑。
- fieldwork：实地研究（如科学/人类学）。		 - He did an internship at a law firm.
- Her nursing practicum was at a clinic.
- The blacksmith took on an apprentice.
- The employee was on probation for 3 months.
- Anthropologists conducted fieldwork in tribes.
crush, mash, pulp, smash, squash - crush：压碎、碾碎（外力使固体变碎，可能保留部分形状）
- mash：捣烂成糊状（多用于食物）
- pulp：打成浆状（完全软烂状态）
- smash：猛力打碎（暴力破坏，碎片飞溅）
- squash：压扁变形（挤压软物体）		 - She crushed the garlic with a knife. - He mashed the bananas for the baby.
- The machine turns wood into pulp for paper.
- He smashed the bottle against the wall.
- She accidentally squashed the tomato.
wasabi, mustard - wasabi：日本芥末（绿色，辣味）。
- mustard：西式芥末（黄色，酸辣）。		 - The sushi was served with wasabi.
- He spread mustard on his sandwich.
sweater, jumper - sweater：(通用) 毛衣、针织衫（AmE/BrE）。
- jumper：(BrE) = 毛衣；(AmE) = 无袖连衣裙（pinafore dress）。		 - He put on a wool sweater.
- BrE: She bought a new jumper.
- AmE: She wore a denim jumper over a T‑shirt.
trousers, pants - trousers：(BrE/正式) 长裤；(AmE) 亦可用但较少。
- pants：(AmE) = 裤子；(BrE) 常指内裤（口语）。		 - AmE: He bought new pants for work.
- BrE: Smart trousers are required; “pants” means underwear.
merchandise, purchase - merchandise：待售商品（集合名词）。
- purchase：v. 购买；n. 购入之物/购买行为。		 - The store displays its merchandise neatly.
- Her latest purchase was a travel adapter.
economic, economical - economic：经济的、与经济学相关的。
- economical：节约的、划算的。		 - The country faces economic challenges.
- An economical car saves fuel.
historic, historical - historic：历史上重要的。
- historical：与历史有关的、基于历史的。		 - A historic summit was held.
- She studies historical documents.
classic, classical - classic：典型的、经典的。
- classical：古典的（音乐、文学、文明）。		 - A classic mistake.
- Classical music calms me.
effective, efficient, effectual - effective：有效达成目标。
- efficient：高效率、少浪费。
- effectual：产生预期效果（常书面，常修饰 remedy/measure）。
- 易错：efficient ≠ fast（强调“投入/产出比”）。		 - The policy is effective.
- This method is more efficient.
- The remedy proved effectual.
affect, effect - affect：v. 影响。
- effect：n. 结果、效果；v. 实现（正式）。		 - The weather can affect crops.
- The effect was significant.
sensible, sensitive, sensual - sensible：明智的。
- sensitive：敏感的（对情绪/刺激）。
- sensual：偏感官享乐、性感。
- 易混：sensitive（人/皮肤/话题），sensible（决定/建议）。		 - A sensible decision.
- Sensitive skin.
- A sensual fragrance.
continual, continuous - continual：反复发生（间断）。
- continuous：连续不断。		 - Continual interruptions.
- A continuous line.
sometime, some time, sometimes - sometime：某时（将来/过去）。
- some time：一段时间。
- sometimes：有时。		 - Let’s meet sometime next week.
- I need some time to think.
- I sometimes read before bed.
principal, principle - principal：最重要的；校长。
- principle：原则。		 - The principal reason.
- Moral principles.
device, devise - device：装置。
- devise：设计、想出。		 - A tracking device.
- Devise a plan.
desert, dessert - desert：沙漠；v. 抛弃。
- dessert：甜点。		 - A vast desert.
- What’s for dessert?
adapt, adopt, adept - adapt：使适应；改编。
- adopt：采纳；收养。
- adept：熟练的（at sth.）。		 - Adapt to change.
- Adopt a new policy.
- He is adept at coding.
compliment, complement - compliment：赞美。
- complement：补充、与…相配。		 - Pay her a compliment.
- The sauce complements the fish.
comprehension, comprehensive, comprehensible - comprehension：理解能力/过程。
- comprehensive：全面的、综合的。
- comprehensible：容易理解的（给读者/听者）。		 - Reading comprehension.
- A comprehensive review.
- The explanation is comprehensible.
considerable, considerate - considerable：相当大的。
- considerate：体贴的。		 - A considerable amount.
- She is very considerate.
site, sight, cite - site：地点/站点（website）。
- sight：视线/景象/名胜。
- cite：引用/引证（学术/法律）。		 - A construction site.
- Out of sight.
- Cite your sources properly.
eminent, imminent, immanent - eminent：杰出的。
- imminent：迫在眉睫的。
- immanent：内在的（如 immanent justice）。		 - An eminent scholar.
- Imminent danger.
- Moral law is immanent in human nature.
farther, further - farther：物理距离更远。
- further：程度/抽象更深；也可指距离（BrE可互换）。		 - The farther shore.
- For further study.
inhibit, prohibit - inhibit：抑制（内部/生物/化学）。
- prohibit：禁止（法律/规则）。		 - Enzymes inhibit growth.
- Smoking is prohibited.
ensure, assure, insure - ensure：确保事情发生。
- assure：向人保证使安心（对象是人）。
- insure：给…投保。		 - Ensure quality.
- I assure you it’s safe.
- Insure your car against theft.
respectively, respectfully - respectively：分别地。
- respectfully：恭敬地。		 - They scored 90 and 92, respectively.
- He bowed respectfully.
imply, infer - imply：说话者暗示。
- infer：听话者推断。		 - Are you implying I’m wrong?
- I inferred he was upset.
injury, wound - injury：受伤（广义）。
- wound：创伤（常由武器造成）。		 - A sports injury.
- A gunshot wound.
disease, illness, sickness - disease：特定疾病（如 diabetes）。
- illness：生病状态/疾病（广）。
- sickness：恶心/生病（口语）。		 - Infectious diseases spread quickly.
- She is on sick leave.
- He has been battling a serious illness.
moral, morale - moral：道德的；寓意。
- morale：士气。		 - Moral values.
- Team morale is high.
liable, likely - liable：有法律责任；易于…的。
- likely：很可能的。		 - He is liable for damages.
- It’s likely to rain.
previous, prior, former - previous：先前的（一般）。
- prior：更早/优先。
- former：前任/从前的。		 - The previous version had bugs.
- Prior notice is required.
- A former president.
despite, in spite of, though - despite/in spite of：介词（后接n./v‑ing）。
- though：连词。		 - Despite the rain, we went out.
- Though it rained, we went out.
between, among - between：两者/明晰个体。
- among：三者及以上/群体。		 - Between you and me.
- Hidden among the trees.
few, a few; little, a little - few/little：几乎没有（否定）。
- a few/a little：有一些（肯定）。		 - Few people agreed.
- I have a little time.
raise, rise, arise - raise：抬起/提高（及物）。
- rise：上升（不及物）。
- arise：出现/产生（抽象）。		 - Raise your hand.
- Prices rise quickly.
- Problems may arise unexpectedly.
remember, recall, remind - remember/recall：记得/回忆。
- remind：提醒某人。		 - I remember his name.
- I recall meeting him once.
- Remind me tomorrow.
famous, notorious, infamous - famous：著名（正）。
- notorious/infamous：臭名昭著（负，infamous更强）。		 - A famous scientist.
- A notorious criminal.
- An infamous scandal.
accident, incident - accident：意外事故（多有伤害）。
- incident：事件（中性/小规模）。		 - A car accident.
- A minor incident.
damp, humid, moist - damp：潮湿（略不舒服）。
- humid：湿热。
- moist：微湿（中性/积极）。		 - The basement felt damp.
- Humid summer air.
- Keep the soil moist.
assume, presume - assume：在缺乏证据下假设。
- presume：有一定依据的推定。		 - Don’t assume the worst.
- I presume you’re right.
guarantee, warranty - guarantee：保证/担保（广义）。
- warranty：保修（商品条款）。		 - We guarantee satisfaction.
- The TV has a 2‑year warranty.
rent, lease, hire - rent：租（通用）。
- lease：长期租约。
- hire：短租（英）；雇用。		 - Rent a room downtown.
- Lease a flat for a year.
- Hire a car for the weekend.
borrow, lend - borrow：借入。
- lend：借出。		 - Can I borrow your pen?
- Could you lend me 10 yuan?
rob, steal, burgle - rob：抢劫某人/机构（抢对象）。
- steal：偷窃物品（偷东西）。
- burgle：(BrE) 入室盗窃。		 - He robbed a bank.
- Someone stole my bike.
- Their house was burgled last night.
say, tell, speak, talk - say：说（内容）。
- tell：告诉（对象）。
- speak/talk：讲话（口语/交流）。		 - She said hello.
- Tell me the truth.
- He spoke at the meeting.
look, see, watch - look：主动看。
- see：被动看到。
- watch：专注观看（动态）。		 - Look at this!
- Did you see that?
- Watch TV.
listen, hear - listen：主动听。
- hear：听到（被动）。		 - Listen carefully.
- Did you hear that?
persuade, convince - persuade：说服去做。
- convince：使信服/相信。		 - Persuade him to stay.
- Convince her it’s safe.
moreover, furthermore, besides - moreover/furthermore：递进补充（正式）。
- besides：此外/而且（口语；亦表“除…之外”）。		 - Moreover, costs fell.
- Besides, it’s late.
cause, lead to, result in - 三者均表“导致”，语体强弱略有差异：cause 更直接；lead to/result in 较中性。 - Smoking can cause cancer.
- Stress may lead to insomnia.
attribute, ascribe - attribute A to B：认为A归因于B（常用）。
- ascribe A to B：较书面。		 - He attributed success to luck.
- Some ascribe the failure to poor planning.
comprise, consist of, be composed of - comprise：包含（整体 comprises 部分）。
- consist of/be composed of：由…组成（被动/静态）。		 - The committee comprises 10 members.
- The team consists of experts.
- The mixture is composed of three gases.
be made of/from/with/by - of：看得出原料；from：看不出原料；with：用作配料/工具；by：被动执行者。 - Wine is made from grapes.
- A table made of wood.
- A salad made with olive oil.
- A novel written by her.
at the end, in the end - at the end：在…末尾（时间/空间）。
- in the end：最终，结果上。		 - At the end of the day.
- In the end, we won.
on time, in time - on time：准时不迟到。
- in time：及时（赶上/来得及）。		 - The train is on time.
- We arrived in time.
due to, owing to, because of - 三者皆可；due to 多作表语/定语；owing to 稍正式；because of 最口语。 - The flight was delayed due to fog.
- Owing to bad weather, the match was canceled.
- It was postponed because of rain.
according to, in accordance with - according to：据…所说/按…。
- in accordance with：依照（正式书面/法规）。		 - According to the report…
- Act in accordance with the law.
restrict, limit, confine - restrict：限制范围/数量。
- limit：设上限。
- confine：禁闭/局限于（主客观）。		 - Laws restrict access.
- Speed limits.
- Confined to bed.
deny, refuse, reject - deny：否认事实/权利。
- refuse：拒绝做/给。
- reject：拒绝接受（论点/申请/提议）。		 - Deny the rumor.
- Refuse to answer.
- Reject the proposal.
scarce, rare - scarce：供不应求。
- rare：罕见的（频率低）。		 - Water is scarce.
- A rare species.
complicated, complex, intricate - complicated：成分多且难；常指步骤/关系。
- complex：结构复杂，系统层面。
- intricate：错综精细（多形容图案/细节）。		 - A complicated procedure.
- A complex system.
- An intricate design.
access, assess - access：访问/获取。
- assess：评估。		 - Access to data.
- Assess the risk.
alternative, alternate - alternative：可供选择的（替代方案）。
- alternate：交替的；隔一个的。		 - An alternative plan.
- Alternate days.
capacity, capability, ability, competence - capacity：容量/潜在能力。
- capability：可执行的能力（设备/组织）。
- ability：个体能力（先天/后天）。
- competence：胜任某岗位/任务。		 - The stadium is at full capacity.
- Technical capability has improved.
- Her ability to work under pressure is high.
- He showed professional competence.
collective, common, joint, mutual - collective：集体的（collective action）。
- common：共同/普通（common sense/common goal）。
- joint：联合的（joint venture）。
- mutual：彼此的（mutual benefit）。		 - Joint effort.
- Mutual respect.
- A collective decision.
- A common interest.
commit, commission, committee - commit：犯（错/罪）；委托；投入。
- commission：委员会；佣金；委任；委托制作。
- committee：委员会（人）。		 - Commit a crime.
- The artist was commissioned to paint a mural.
- A research committee.
compensation, compensate - compensation：赔偿/补偿。
- `compensate** for**：对…进行补偿。		 - Seek compensation for loss.
- The company will compensate you for damages.
compete, competition, competence, competitive, competitor - compete：竞争。
- competition：竞争/比赛。
- competence：胜任。
- competitive：有竞争力的。
- competitor：竞争者。		 - Firms compete for talent.
- The competition was fierce.
- A competitive salary.
- A strong competitor in the market.
comply, complain, compliment, complement - comply：遵从（with）。
- complain：抱怨（about）。
- compliment：恭维。
- complement：补足、与…相得益彰。		 - Comply with rules.
- He complained about the noise.
- Accept a compliment.
- The wine complements the steak.
discipline, subject, major - discipline：学科；纪律。
- subject：课程/主题。
- major：主修专业。		 - Physics is his major.
- Linguistics is an academic discipline.
- This subject is challenging.
dispose, disposal, dispose of - dispose：安排/处理（正式）。
- disposal：处置（at one’s disposal = 可支配）。
- dispose of：丢弃/清除。		 - Dispose of waste properly.
- I have several tools at my disposal.
evident, evidential, evidence - evident：明显的。
- evidential：证据的/根据证据的（evidential support）。
- evidence：证据。		 - Evident errors.
- Evidential standards are required.
- Evidence supports it.
fiction, fictional, fictitious - fiction：虚构文学。
- fictional：虚构的（作品中的）。
- fictitious：假的、杜撰的。		 - She prefers science fiction.
- A fictional character.
- A fictitious name.
fragile, brittle - fragile：易碎/脆弱（整体）。
- brittle：脆性、易裂。		 - Fragile glass.
- Brittle bones.
forbid, ban, prohibit - forbid：个人/权威禁止。
- ban：官方明令禁止。
- prohibit：法律法规禁止。		 - The school forbids smoking.
- The drug is banned.
fraction, fragment - fraction：分数/部分。
- fragment：碎片。		 - A fraction of the cost.
- Glass fragments.
hinder, hamper, impede, obstruct - 强度：hinder/hamper < impede < obstruct（完全阻断）。 - Traffic impeded by snow.
- A view obstructed by trees.
illusion, delusion - illusion：错觉。
- delusion：妄想（精神/固执的错误信念）。		 - Optical illusion.
- He suffers from delusions.
induce, deduce - induce：引起/诱导。
- deduce：推断。		 - Induce sleep.
- Deduce from facts.
intense, intensive - intense：强烈的。
- intensive：密集的、高强度的。		 - Intense heat.
- Intensive training.
inspect, investigate - inspect：检查（常规/细查）。
- investigate：调查（原因/真相）。		 - Inspect equipment.
- Investigate a case.
instant, instantaneous, instance - instant：即时；瞬间。
- instantaneous：瞬时的。
- instance：实例。		 - In an instant.
- The response was almost instantaneous.
- For instance…
install, instill - install：安装。
- instill：逐渐灌输。		 - Install software.
- Instill confidence.
latter, later, latest - latter：后者。
- later：更晚。
- latest：最新的。		 - Choose the latter.
- We’ll talk later.
- The latest news.
maintain, remain - maintain：维持/保养；主张。
- remain：保持/留下。		 - Maintain order.
- Remain calm.
medium, median, average - medium：中等；媒介。
- median：中位数/中央分隔带。
- average：平均。		 - A medium size T‑shirt.
- The median value is 10.
- The average score rose.
pattern, mode, method - pattern：模式（重复规律）。
- mode：方式/状态（flight mode/learning mode）。
- method：方法（有步骤）。		 - A sleeping pattern.
- Switch to airplane mode.
- The scientific method.
period, phase, stage - period：时期（广）。
- phase：阶段（过渡）。
- stage：阶段（步骤/舞台）。		 - The period of rapid growth.
- The project is in its testing phase.
- The final stage.
phenomenon, phenomenal - phenomenon：现象。
- phenomenal：非凡的。		 - A rare phenomenon.
- Phenomenal success.
policy, police, politics - policy：政策。
- police：警察（集合名词）。
- politics：政治学/政治活动（不可数）。		 - Foreign policy.
- The police are investigating.
- He studies politics.
potential, potent - potential：潜在的/潜力。
- potent：有力的/有效的。		 - Potential risks.
- A potent drug.
prejudice, bias, discrimination - prejudice：成见（态度）。
- bias：偏见/偏向（统计偏差/倾向）。
- discrimination：歧视（行为）。		 - Racial discrimination is illegal.
- Media bias is a concern.
- Deep‑rooted prejudice persists.
prominent, predominant - prominent：突出的/杰出的。
- predominant：占主导的。		 - A prominent figure.
- The predominant view.
prospect, perspective - prospect：前景/可能性。
- perspective：观点/透视。		 - Career prospects.
- From my perspective…
psychology, psychiatry - psychology：心理学。
- psychiatry：精神病学。		 - Study psychology.
- Practice psychiatry.
reform, transform - reform：改革（改善）。
- transform：彻底改变。		 - Reform the system.
- Transform society.
regulate, legislate - regulate：监管、调节。
- legislate：立法。		 - Legislate for safety.
- Regulate emissions.
relief, relieve, release - relief：解脱/救济。
- relieve：缓解（relieve pain/stress）。
- release：释放/发布。		 - Pain relief.
- This medicine can relieve headache.
- Release a report.
respective, respectable, respectful - respective：各自的。
- respectable：体面的/值得尊敬的。
- respectful：恭敬的。		 - Go to their respective rooms.
- A respectable income.
- A respectful attitude.
salary, wage, pay - salary：固定月薪（白领）。
- wage：按时/日/周（蓝领）。
- pay：泛指薪酬。		 - A competitive salary.
- Minimum wage rose.
- Equal pay for equal work.
scarce, scarcely - scarce：稀缺的（形容词）。
- scarcely：几乎不（副词）。		 - Food is scarce.
- I could scarcely believe it.
secure, ensure, insure, assure - secure：获得/保全（secure funding）。
- ensure：确保某事发生。
- insure：投保。
- assure：向人保证使其安心。		 - Secure funding.
- Ensure compliance.
- Insure your car.
- I assure you it will work.
statistics, statistician - statistics：统计学/统计数据。
- statistician：统计学家。		 - Study statistics.
- A statistician analyzed it.
strategy, tactics - strategy：战略（长期）。
- tactics：战术（短期手段）。		 - Military strategy and tactics.
substance, subject - substance：物质/主旨。
- subject：主题/学科/主体。		 - The substance of his speech.
- The subject of today’s lecture is AI.
substantial, substantive - substantial：大量的/实质性的。
- substantive：实质上的/独立的（语法/法律）。		 - Substantial progress.
- A substantive change.
succeed, successive, successor, successful - succeed：成功/继任。
- successive：连续的。
- successor：继任者。
- successful：成功的。		 - She succeeded in passing CET‑6.
- Successive wins boosted morale.
- Her successor took office.
- A successful campaign.
susceptible, vulnerable - susceptible to：易受影响/感染。
- vulnerable to：易受伤害/攻击。		 - Susceptible to flu.
- Vulnerable groups.
sympathy, empathy - sympathy：同情（为他人难过）。
- empathy：共情（设身处地）。		 - Feel sympathy for victims.
- Show empathy.
temper, temperament - temper：脾气（易怒/控制）；回火（材料）。
- temperament：气质。		 - He has a bad temper.
- An artistic temperament.
temporary, temporal, contemporary - temporary：临时的。
- temporal：时间的/世俗的（对比 spiritual）。
- contemporary：同时代的/当代的。		 - Temporary staff.
- Temporal lobe.
- Contemporary art.
vary, alter, change - vary：变化（在范围内不同）。
- alter：改变（部分）。
- change：改变（广）。		 - Prices vary by region.
- Alter the plan slightly.
- Change your mind.
virtual, virtue, virtually - virtual：虚拟的/实质上的。
- virtue：美德。
- virtually：几乎。		 - Virtual reality.
- Patience is a virtue.
- Virtually impossible.
voyage, journey, trip, tour, travel - voyage：海/空长途。
- journey：旅行过程。
- trip：短途出行。
- tour：巡游/观光。
- travel：旅行（总称/动词）。		 - A long sea voyage.
- A journey across China.
- A business trip.
- A world tour.
- He loves to travel.
while, whereas, whilst - while/whereas：然而/对照（连词）。
- whilst：英式、较正式。		 - While some agree, others object.
- Whereas others disagree…
- Whilst I see your point, I differ.
weary, wary - weary：疲倦的。
- wary：警惕的。		 - Weary travelers.
- Be wary of scams.
yield, reap, harvest - yield：产出/屈服。
- reap：收获（抽象/庄稼）。
- harvest：收割（具体）。		 - Yield better results.
- Reap the benefits of hard work.
- Harvest wheat.
zone, area, region, district - zone：分区（功能）。
- area：区域（广）。
- region：地区（较大）。
- district：行政区。		 - A no‑parking zone.
- A residential area.
- The northern region.
- A school district.
heroin, heroine - heroin： heroin（毒品）。
- heroine： heroine（女 heroin 用户）。		 - Heroin is a drug.
- Heroine is a female user.
