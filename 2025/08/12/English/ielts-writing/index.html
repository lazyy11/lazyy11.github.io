雅思写作高分指南（超详细版）

本指南为每一类题型和话题提供了详尽的写作指导、句型模板和核心词汇，旨在帮助备考者，特别是初学者，快速掌握雅思写作的核心技巧，并写出结构清晰、内容充实、语言地道的文章。

第一部分：雅思写作官方评分标准详解

要获得高分，首先必须理解评分者是如何评判你的文章的。雅思写作评分有四项标准，每项各占25%。

大作文 (Task 2) 评分标准

任务回应 (Task Response - TR)

核心要求：是否全面、准确、深入地回应了题目的所有部分。

如何提分：

  • 不偏题、不漏题：仔细阅读题目，识别所有关键词和任务要求（如"discuss both views AND give your opinion"）。
  • 观点清晰：在引言和结论中明确表达你的立场。
  • 论证充分：主体段需要有清晰的中心句，并围绕它进行充分的解释和举例。
  • 避免绝对化：使用如 tend to, may, often 等词语，避免使用 all, never 等绝对词。

连贯与衔接 (Coherence and Cohesion - CC)

核心要求：文章结构是否清晰，逻辑是否流畅，段落与句子之间衔接是否自然。

如何提分：

  • 段落划分：遵循四段式结构，每段一个中心思想。
  • 逻辑连接词：恰当使用连接词（如 However, Therefore, In addition）来表明逻辑关系，但不要过度使用。
  • 信息指代：使用代词（it, they, this issue）来指代前文提到的内容，避免重复。
  • 平行结构：使用平行结构使句子更有节奏感和清晰度。

词汇资源 (Lexical Resource - LR)

核心要求：词汇使用的广度、准确性和恰当性。

如何提分：

  • 同义词替换：避免重复使用简单词汇（如 important -> crucial, vital, essential）。
  • 搭配准确：注意词语之间的搭配（如 commit a crime, raise awareness）。
  • 话题词汇：使用与特定话题相关的词汇。
  • 拼写准确：尽量避免拼写错误。

语法范围与准确性 (Grammatical Range and Accuracy - GRA)

核心要求：句型结构的多样性和语法的准确性。

如何提分：

  • 句型多样：混合使用简单句、复合句和复杂句（包含定语从句、状语从句等）。
  • 高级结构：尝试使用倒装句、强调句、被动语态等。
  • 语法准确：注意主谓一致、时态、冠词、介词等常见语法点。
  • 标点正确：正确使用逗号、句号等标点符号。

小作文 (Task 1) 评分标准

任务完成 (Task Achievement - TA)

核心要求：是否准确、全面地描述了图表中的所有关键信息，并进行了必要的对比。

如何提分：

  • 关键信息：找出并描述图表的总体趋势、最大值、最小值、转折点和重要对比。
  • 数据支撑：所有描述都必须有图表中的数据支持。
  • 概述段：必须有一个清晰的概述段（Overview）来总结主要特征。
  • 客观描述：不要加入任何个人观点或图表未提供的信息。

连贯与衔接 (Coherence and Cohesion - CC)

核心要求：信息组织是否合理，逻辑是否清晰。

如何提分：

  • 信息分组：将相关信息（如相似趋势的线、占比最大的几项）放在同一段落描述。
  • 逻辑清晰：遵循"引言-概述-细节1-细节2"的结构。
  • 连接词：使用连接词（如 In contrast, Similarly）来连接不同的数据点。

词汇资源 (Lexical Resource - LR)

核心要求：描述数据和趋势的词汇是否准确、多样。

如何提分：

  • 趋势词汇：熟练运用描述上升、下降、波动、平稳的词汇。
  • 程度词汇：使用副词（如 dramatically, steadily, slightly）来描述变化的程度。
  • 比例词汇：掌握描述比例、分数的词汇（如 accounted for, constituted）。

语法范围与准确性 (Grammatical Range and Accuracy - GRA)

核心要求：与大作文相同，注重句型多样和语法准确。

如何提分：

  • 时态准确：如果图表描述的是过去的时间，通常使用过去时。
  • 句型变换：使用不同的句式来表达相同的数据，如 “The figure for X was 50%” 和 “X accounted for 50%”.

第二部分：通用高分技巧与策略

1. 语法多样性指导

要突破6分，必须展示你运用复杂语法结构的能力。

复合句与复杂句：

技巧：使用 and, but, so 连接两个简单句构成复合句；使用从属连词（如 although, because, while, when）引导从句，构成复杂句。

示例：

  • “The government has invested in public transport, but traffic congestion remains a serious problem.” (复合句)
  • “Although the government has invested in public transport, traffic congestion remains a serious problem.” (复杂句)

三大从句的应用：

定语从句：用来修饰名词，由 who, which, that 等引导。

  • “Many people who live in large cities face high living costs.”

状语从句：用来修饰动词或整个句子，表示时间、原因、条件、让步等。

  • “As technology develops, online education has become increasingly popular.”

名词性从句：在句子中充当主语、宾语、表语或同位语。

  • “What we need to do is to raise public awareness.” (主语从句)

高级句型：

倒装句：“Not only do cities provide more job opportunities, but they also offer better educational resources.”

强调句：“It is the government that should take the primary responsibility for environmental protection.”

被动语态：在描述客观事实或流程图时常用。“More attention should be paid to mental health issues.”

如何避免常见语法错误：

  • 主谓一致：确保主语和谓语动词在单复数上保持一致。
  • 时态一致：全文时态应根据语境保持逻辑一致。
  • 冠词与介词：这是中国考生的老大难问题，需要通过多读多记来积累。

2. 论证深度拓展 (大作文)

高分作文不仅要有观点，更要有深度。

分析因果关系：

不要只说"现象A发生了"，要深入探讨"为什么会发生A（原因）“以及"A会导致什么（结果）”。

示例（表面）：“More and more people are suffering from obesity.”

示例（深入）：“The prevalence of sedentary lifestyles, coupled with the easy access to high-calorie fast food, has led to a surge in obesity rates, which in turn places a heavy burden on the public healthcare system.”

多角度思考：

从不同层面分析一个问题，能让你的论证更全面。

  • 个人层面：对个人的生活、健康、心理有何影响？
  • 社会层面：对社会结构、文化、公共服务有何影响？
  • 经济层面：对就业、经济增长、资源分配有何影响？
  • 环境层面：对生态系统、资源保护有何影响？

恰当的论证方法：

  • 举例论证：用具体的、有代表性的例子来支撑观点。
  • 对比论证：通过对比两种不同情况来突出观点。
  • 因果论证：分析问题的原因和结果。
  • 分类论证：将复杂问题分解成几个方面来分别讨论。

3. 时间管理策略

严格的时间管理是完成两篇作文的前提。

大作文 (40分钟)

  • 审题和规划 (5分钟)：仔细读题，圈出关键词，快速列出文章结构和各段的中心思想。
  • 写作 (30分钟)：快速完成四个段落的写作。不要在某个词或句子上纠结太久。
  • 检查 (5分钟)：检查语法、拼写和标点错误。这是提分的关键步骤。

小作文 (20分钟)

  • 审题和分析 (3-5分钟)：读懂图表，找出总体趋势和关键数据点。
  • 写作 (12-15分钟)：快速写完四段。
  • 检查 (2-3分钟)：检查数据是否准确，语法和拼写是否有误。

4. 常见错误分析

大作文常见错误：

  • 偏题/跑题：未能准确理解题目，导致回答不相关。
  • 论证不足：只有观点，没有解释和例子。
  • 例子不当：例子过于个人化或不具代表性。
  • 观点模糊：在"同意与否"或"双边讨论"中没有清晰表达自己的立场。

小作文常见错误：

  • 遗漏关键信息：没有写"概述段"，或遗漏了图表的主要特征。
  • 数据描述不准确：抄错数据或单位。
  • 个人观点过多：对数据进行主观臆测和评价。
  • 简单罗列数据：缺乏对比和信息组织，像流水账。

语言常见错误 (中式英语)：

  • 冠词/介词使用：on the picture (应为 in the picture)。
  • 时态混淆：描述过去图表时使用现在时。
  • 冗余表达：I, personally, in my opinion, think that… (过于冗长)。

5. 实用技巧与策略

审题技巧：

  • 识别关键词：圈出题目中的核心名词、动词和限定词。
  • 确定任务：明确题目要求是"利弊"、"同意与否"还是其他类型。

规划技巧 (列提纲)：

  • 引言：准备如何改写题目和表达观点。
  • 主体段1：中心句 + 1-2个支撑点。
  • 主体段2：中心句 + 1-2个支撑点。
  • 结论：准备如何总结观点。

检查技巧：

  • 从后往前读：这样可以让你更专注于单个单词和句子，更容易发现错误。
  • 重点检查项：主谓一致、时态、单复数、拼写。

临场应变：

  • 遇到不熟悉话题：不要慌张。利用多角度思考法（社会、经济、个人等）来构思观点。雅思话题通常是普适性的，不需要专业知识。

6. 针对不同水平考生的建议

基础较弱 (目标5.5分及以下)：

  • 首要任务：确保文章结构完整，完成写作任务。
  • 重点：掌握基本句型（主谓宾），确保主谓一致和时态正确。背诵并熟练使用各话题下的基础词汇。

中等水平 (目标6-6.5分)：

  • 首要任务：提高词汇和语法的多样性。
  • 重点：学习使用复合句和简单从句。积累同义词和话题词汇。加强段落间的逻辑连接。

较高水平 (目标7分及以上)：

  • 首要任务：追求论证深度和语言的精准地道。
  • 重点：培养批判性思维，提出有见地的观点。使用更复杂、更自然的句型结构。注意词语搭配的恰当性，避免中式英语。

第三部分：大作文 (Task 2) 写作攻略

雅思大作文要求在40分钟内写一篇不少于250词的议论文。关键在于结构清晰、论证有力、语言准确。

通用四段式结构 (包含论证与衔接技巧)

大多数大作文都可以套用经典的四段式结构，这是最稳妥、最清晰的结构。

第一段：引言 (Introduction)

  • 背景句 (Hook)：引用一个普遍现象或社会背景，引出话题。
  • 改写题目 (Paraphrase)：用自己的话转述题目中的观点或问题。
  • 表明立场/概述文章 (Thesis Statement)：清晰地表达你的观点（如果题目要求），或者说明你将要讨论哪几个方面。

第二段：主体段 1 (Body Paragraph 1)

  • 中心句 (Topic Sentence)：清晰陈述本段的核心论点。
  • 解释 (Explanation)：详细阐述中心句，解释为什么会有这个论点。
  • 举例/论证 (Example/Support)：给出具体的例子、数据或进一步的逻辑推理来支撑你的论点。
  • 段内衔接技巧：
    • 逻辑词：使用 For instance, Specifically 来引出例子；使用 As a result, Consequently 来解释后果。
    • 代词指代：使用 this trend, such a problem 来连接句子，使段落更流畅。

第三段：主体段 2 (Body Paragraph 2)

  • 中心句 (Topic Sentence)：陈述另一个核心论点（可以是反方观点，也可以是递进论点）。
  • 解释 (Explanation)：详细阐述该论点。
  • 举例/论证 (Example/Support)：提供支撑。
  • 段内衔接技巧：
    • 逻辑词：使用 For instance, Specifically 来引出例子；使用 As a result, Consequently 来解释后果。
    • 代词指代：使用 this trend, such a problem 来连接句子，使段落更流畅。

第四段：结论 (Conclusion)

  • 重申立场 (Restate Thesis)：再次总结你的核心观点。
  • 总结论点 (Summarize)：简要概括两个主体段的主要内容。
  • 升华/建议 (Final Thought/Suggestion)：（可选）可以提出一个展望、建议或一个引人深思的结尾。

五大题型模板详解

1. 利弊比较 (Advantages and Disadvantages)

题目形式：“Discuss the advantages and disadvantages of…”, “Do the advantages outweigh the disadvantages?”

核心任务：客观分析事物的两面性，并（根据题目要求）给出权衡后的结论。

模板：

  • 引言：背景句（引出某现象）+ 改写题目 + 表明需权衡利弊。
    示例：“Nowadays, [某现象] is becoming increasingly common. While it brings certain benefits, there are also drawbacks that need to be considered.”
  • 主体段1 (优点)：“On the one hand, there are several notable advantages.” + 首先 (Firstly), … (解释+举例) + 其次 (Secondly), … (解释+举例).
  • 主体段2 (缺点)：“On the other hand, the disadvantages cannot be ignored.” + 一个主要缺点是 (A major drawback is that)… (解释+举例) + 此外 (Furthermore), … (解释+举例).
  • 结论：“In conclusion, while [某事物] offers benefits such as [优点1] and [优点2], its negative impacts like [缺点1] and [缺点2] are also significant.” + (如果被问到) “In my opinion, the advantages outweigh the disadvantages, provided that… (优点大于缺点，前提是…)”

常用句式、连接词与过渡句：

  • 开头段：
    • “While I accept that… I believe that…”
    • “It is true that… While I understand that critics may see this as an invasion of privacy, I believe that there are more benefits than drawbacks.”
  • 主体段：
    • “On the one hand,…”
    • “The main problem for anyone who hopes to… is that…”
    • “Another issue is that…”
    • “Furthermore,…”
    • “As a result,…”
    • “However, I would argue that these benefits are outweighed by the drawbacks.”
    • “Firstly,… Secondly,… A third option would be to…”
    • 词汇替换：“drawbacks” 替换 “disadvantages”；“inhabitants of cities” 替换 “people who lived in the cities”；“urban areas” 替换 “cities”。

范文示例：
题目：Some people regard video games as harmless fun, or even as a useful educational tool. Others, however, believe that videos games are having an adverse effect on the people who play them. In your opinion, do the drawbacks of video games outweigh the benefits?

范文：
Many people, children in particular, enjoy playing computer games. While I accept that these games can sometimes have a positive effect on the user, I believe that they are more likely to have a harmful impact.

On the one hand, video games can be both entertaining and educational. Users, or gamers, are transported into virtual worlds which are often more exciting and engaging than real-life pastimes. From an educational perspective, these games encourage imagination and creativity, as well as concentration, logical thinking and problem solving, all of which are useful skills outside the gaming context. Furthermore, it has been shown that computer simulation games can improve users’ motor skills and help to prepare them for real-world tasks, such as flying a plane.

However, I would argue that these benefits are outweighed by the drawbacks. Gaming can be highly addictive because users are constantly given scores, new targets and frequent rewards to keep them playing. Many children now spend hours each day trying to progress through the levels of a game or to get a higher score than their friends. This type of addiction can have effects ranging from lack of sleep to problems at school, when homework is sacrificed for a few more hours on the computer or console. The rise in obesity in recent years has also been linked in part to the sedentary lifestyle and lack of exercise that often accompany gaming addiction.

In conclusion, it seems to me that the potential dangers of video games are more significant than the possible benefits.

2. 同意与否 (Agree or Disagree)

题目形式：“To what extent do you agree or disagree?”, “Do you think…?”

核心任务：明确表达你的立场（完全同意、完全不同意、部分同意），并提供充分的理由。

模板 (一边倒)：

  • 引言：背景句 + 改写题目 + 明确立场。
    示例：“It is true that [题目观点]. Personally, I completely agree/disagree with this statement.”
  • 主体段1 (理由一)：“The primary reason for my stance is that…” + (解释+举例).
  • 主体段2 (理由二)：“Another compelling reason is that…” + (解释+举例).
  • 结论：“In summary, due to [理由1] and [理由2], I am convinced that…” + (重申观点).

模板 (折中/部分同意)：

  • 引言：改写题目 + 承认部分合理性 + 明确核心观点。
    示例：“It is true that [题目观点]. While I accept that [部分合理之处], I believe that [核心观点].”
  • 主体段1 (承认对方观点)：“Admittedly, it is true that [对方观点的合理之处].” + (解释+举例).
  • 主体段2 (阐述你的主要观点)：“However, I would argue that [你的核心论点] is more significant.” + (解释+举例).
  • 结论：“To conclude, although there is some merit to the idea that [承认的观点], I hold the view that [你的主要观点] is a more accurate reflection of the situation.”

常用句式、连接词与过渡句：

  • 开头段：
    • 一边倒：“It is true that… I completely agree/disagree with the idea that…”。
    • 折中观点：“It is true that… While I agree that…, I believe that others are still relevant/useful/enjoy changing careers/seeking job satisfaction in different ways.”。
    • “In my opinion, this trend is having both positive and negative consequences in equal measure.”
  • 主体段：
    • “In my opinion,…”
    • “For example,…”
    • “On the one hand,… On the other hand,…”
    • “Firstly,… Secondly,… Finally,…”
    • “As a result,…”
    • “By contrast,…”
    • “Nevertheless,…”
    • “Furthermore,…”
  • 总结段：
    • “In conclusion, I fully support the view that…”
    • “In conclusion, although… we should not dismiss all traditional ideas as irrelevant.”
    • “In conclusion, it can certainly be satisfying to pursue a particular career for the whole of one’s life, but this is by no means the only route to fulfillment.”

范文示例（折中观点）：
题目：The older generations tend to have very traditional ideas about how people should live, think and behave. However, some people believe that these ideas are not helpful in preparing younger generations for modern life. To what extent do you agree or disagree with this view?

范文：
It is true that many older people believe in traditional values that often seem incompatible with the needs of younger people. While I agree that some traditional ideas are outdated, I believe that others are still useful and should not be forgotten.

On the one hand, many of the ideas that elderly people have about life are becoming less relevant for younger people. In the past, for example, people were advised to learn a profession and find a secure job for life, but today’s workers expect much more variety and diversity from their careers. At the same time, the ‘rules’ around relationships are being eroded as young adults make their own choices about who and when to marry. But perhaps the greatest disparity between the generations can be seen in their attitudes towards gender roles. The traditional roles of men and women, as breadwinners and housewives, are no longer accepted as necessary or appropriate by most younger people.

On the other hand, some traditional views and values are certainly applicable to the modern world. For example, older generations attach great importance to working hard, doing one’s best, and taking pride in one’s work, and these behaviours can surely benefit young people as they enter today’s competitive job market. Other characteristics that are perhaps seen as traditional are politeness and good manners. In our globalized world, young adults can expect to come into contact with people from a huge variety of backgrounds, and it is more important than ever to treat others with respect. Finally, I believe that young people would lead happier lives if they had a more “old-fashioned” sense of community and neighborliness.

In conclusion, although the views of older people may sometimes seem unhelpful in today’s world, we should not dismiss all traditional ideas as irrelevant.

3. 双边讨论 (Discuss Both Views)

题目形式：“Discuss both these views and give your own opinion.”

核心任务：分别阐述两种对立的观点，然后给出你自己的看法。

模板：

  • 引言：引出话题核心 + 概述两种对立观点 + 明确自己的立场。
    示例：“People have different views about [话题核心]. Some argue that [观点一内容], while others claim that [观点二内容]. In my opinion, [你的立场：更倾向观点一 / 观点二 / 折中看法].”
  • 主体段1 (观点一)：“On the one hand, there are valid reasons why some people believe that [观点一].” + (解释+举例).
  • 主体段2 (观点二)：“On the other hand, others argue that [观点二].” + (解释+举例).
  • 结论：“In conclusion, both sides of the argument have their merits. While [观点一] is understandable in the context of [某种情况], I personally lean towards the belief that [观点二] because [你的理由].”

常用句式、连接词与过渡句：

  • 开头段：
    • “People have different views about whether… Although there are some advantages of…, I believe that…”
    • “People have different views about whether governments should introduce a maximum wage. While in some ways it may seem reasonable to allow people to earn as much as companies are willing to pay, I personally believe that employee remuneration should be capped at a certain level.”
  • 主体段：
    • “On the one hand, there are clear ethical arguments against…”
    • “To use a common example of this practice,…”
    • “Opponents of such research argue that…”
    • “On the other hand, reliable alternatives to… may not always be available.”
    • “Supporters of the use of… believe that…”
    • “Personally, I agree with… but I feel that it may be a necessary evil where…”
    • “There are various reasons why it might be considered beneficial to allow people to be paid extremely high salaries.”
    • “For example,… Furthermore,… In theory, this should result in…”
    • “However, I agree with those who argue that…”
    • “By introducing a limit on earnings,…”
    • “One possible consequence of greater equality could be that…”
  • 总结段：
    • “In conclusion, it seems to me that it would be wrong to ban testing on animals for vital medical research until equally effective alternatives have been developed.”
    • “In conclusion, it seems to me that it would be better, on balance, for governments to set a limit on the wages of the highest earners in society.”

范文示例：
题目：Nowadays animal experiments are widely used to develop new medicines and to test the safety of other products. Some people argue that these experiments should be banned because it is morally wrong to cause animals to suffer, while others are in favour of them because of their benefits to humanity. Discuss both views and give your own opinion.

范文：
It is true that medicines and other products are routinely tested on animals before they are cleared for human use. While I tend towards the viewpoint that animal testing is morally wrong, I would have to support a limited amount of animal experimentation for the development of medicines.

On the one hand, there are clear ethical arguments against animal experimentation. To use a common example of this practice, laboratory mice may be given an illness so that the effectiveness of a new drug can be measured. Opponents of such research argue that humans have no right to subject animals to this kind of trauma, and that the lives of all creatures should be respected. They believe that the benefits to humans do not justify the suffering caused, and that scientists should use alternative methods of research.

On the other hand, reliable alternatives to animal experimentation may not always be available. Supporters of the use of animals in medical research believe that a certain amount of suffering on the part of mice or rats can be justified if human lives are saved. They argue that opponents of such research might feel differently if a member of their own families needed a medical treatment that had been developed through the use of animal experimentation. Personally, I agree with the banning of animal testing for non-medical products, but I feel that it may be a necessary evil where new drugs and medical procedures are concerned.

In conclusion, it seems to me that it would be wrong to ban testing on animals for vital medical research until equally effective alternatives have been developed.

4. 问题与对策 (Problems and Solutions)

题目形式：“What are the causes/problems…? What are some possible solutions?”

核心任务：分析某个问题产生的原因，并提出可行的解决方案。

模板：

  • 引言：点明问题的普遍性 + 引出原因分析和对策探讨。
    示例：“[某问题] has become a growing concern in recent years. There are several reasons for this, and various measures could be taken to address it.”
  • 主体段1 (原因/问题)：“Several factors contribute to this worrying trend.” + 首先 (Firstly), [原因1] (解释+举例) + 其次 (Secondly), [原因2] (解释+举例).
  • 主体段2 (对策)：“To tackle this problem, a combination of measures should be implemented.” + 对政府而言 (For governments), … (提出具体措施) + 对个人而言 (For individuals), … (提出具体措施).
  • 结论：“In conclusion, the problem of [问题], which stems from [原因1] and [原因2], can be effectively addressed if governments and individuals work together to [总结方案].”

常用句式、连接词与过渡句：

  • 开头段：
    • “It is undeniable that… There are several causes of this alarming trend, but measures could certainly be taken to tackle the problem.”
    • “Cities are often seen as places of opportunity, but there are also some major drawbacks of living in a large metropolis. In my opinion, governments could do much more to improve city life for the average inhabitant.”
  • 主体段：
    • “The main problem for anyone who hopes to migrate to a large city is that…”
    • “Another issue is that urban areas tend to suffer from social problems such as…”
    • “Furthermore, the air quality in cities is often poor, due to…”
    • “As a result, city life can be unhealthy and stressful.”
    • “However, there are various steps that governments could take to tackle these problems.”
    • “Firstly, they could invest money in…”
    • “Secondly, politicians have the power to…”
    • “A third option would be to develop…”
    • 词汇替换：如将“difficulties”替换为“drawbacks”。

范文示例：
题目：More and more people are migrating to cities in search of a better life, but city life can be extremely difficult. Explain some of the difficulties of living in a city. How can governments make urban life better for everyone?

范文：
Cities are often seen as places of opportunity, but there are also some major drawbacks of living in a large metropolis. In my opinion, governments could do much more to improve city life for the average inhabitant.

The main problem for anyone who hopes to migrate to a large city is that the cost of living is likely to be much higher than it is in a small town or village. Inhabitants of cities have to pay higher prices for housing, transport, and even food. Another issue is that urban areas tend to suffer from social problems such as high crime and poverty rates in comparison with rural areas. Furthermore, the air quality in cities is often poor, due to pollution from traffic, and the streets and public transport systems are usually overcrowded. As a result, city life can be unhealthy and stressful.

However, there are various steps that governments could take to tackle these problems. Firstly, they could invest money in the building of affordable or social housing to reduce the cost of living. Secondly, politicians have the power to ban vehicles from city centres and promote the use of cleaner public transport, which would help to reduce both air pollution and traffic congestion. In London, for example, the introduction of a congestion charge for drivers has helped to curb the traffic problem. A third option would be to develop provincial towns and rural areas, by moving industry and jobs to those regions, in order to reduce the pressure on major cities.

In conclusion, governments could certainly implement a range of measures to enhance the quality of life for all city residents.

5. 混合题型 (Mixed Questions)

题目形式：通常是两种题型的结合，如 “Why is this the case? Is this a positive or negative development?”

核心任务：仔细审题，确保回答了题目的每一个部分。

模板 (原因+利弊)：

  • 引言：引出现象 + 衔接原因分析和利弊评估。
    示例：“[现象] is becoming more prevalent, and there are several reasons for this. Whether this is a positive development depends on various factors.”
  • 主体段1 (原因)：“There are several reasons for this development.” + (分别阐述原因).
  • 主体段2 (利弊评估)：“In my view, this trend is largely positive/negative.” + (阐述是积极还是消极，并给出理由和例子).
  • 结论：“In summary, while this trend is driven by [原因总结], its overall impact is [积极/消极], and therefore should be [鼓励/警惕].”

常用句式、连接词与过渡句：

  • 开头段：
    • “It is no doubt true that… While the personal nature of happiness makes it difficult to describe, there do seem to be some common needs that we all share with regard to experiencing or achieving happiness.”
    • “It is true that men are increasingly likely to take on the role of househusband, while more women than ever are the breadwinners in their families. There could be several reasons for this, and I consider it to be a very positive trend.”
  • 主体段：
    • “Happiness is difficult to define because it means something different to each individual person.”
    • “Some people, for example, derive a sense of satisfaction from… whereas for others,…”
    • “Although it seems almost impossible to give a precise definition of happiness, most people would agree that there are some basic preconditions to achieving it. Firstly,… Secondly,… Other key factors could be…”
    • “In recent years, parents have had to adapt to various changes in our societies.”
    • “Equal rights movements have made great progress, and it has become normal for…”
    • “At the same time, the rising cost of living has meant that…”
    • “In my view, the changes described above should be seen as progress.”
    • “We should be happy to live in a society in which… and in which…”
    • “Equally, it seems only fair that…”
    • “Couples should be left to make their own decisions about…”
    • “On an individual level,… From an economic perspective,…”
    • “However, the personal and economic arguments given above can be considered from the opposite angle.”
  • 总结段：
    • “In conclusion, happiness is difficult to define because it is particular to each individual, but I believe that our basic needs for shelter, food and company need to be fulfilled before we can experience it.”
    • “In conclusion, the changing roles of men and women in the family are a result of wider changes in society, and I believe that these developments are desirable.”

范文示例：
题目：These days more fathers stay at home and take care of their children while mothers go out to work. What could be the reasons for this? Do you think it is a positive or a negative development?

范文：
It is true that men are increasingly likely to take on the role of househusband, while more women than ever are the breadwinners in their families. There could be several reasons for this, and I consider it to be a very positive trend.

In recent years, parents have had to adapt to various changes in our societies. Equal rights movements have made great progress, and it has become normal for women to gain qualifications and pursue a career. It has also become socially acceptable for men to stay at home and look after their children. At the same time, the rising cost of living has meant that both marriage partners usually need to work and save money before starting a family. Therefore, when couples have children, they may decide who works and who stays at home depending on the personal preference of each partner, or based on which partner earns the most money.

In my view, the changes described above should be seen as progress. We should be happy to live in a society in which men and women have equal opportunities, and in which women are not put under pressure to sacrifice their careers. Equally, it seems only fair that men should be free to leave their jobs in order to assume childcare responsibilities if this is what they wish to do. Couples should be left to make their own decisions about which parental role each partner takes, according to their particular circumstances and needs.

In conclusion, the changing roles of men and women in the family are a result of wider changes in society, and I believe that these developments are desirable.

高级词汇与批判性思维

替换常见词汇：

  • bad -> detrimental, harmful, negative
  • think -> argue, claim, contend, believe
  • show -> illustrate, demonstrate, reveal

培养批判性思维：

  • 评估观点：不仅要陈述观点，还要思考其合理性和局限性。
  • 避免二元对立：很多问题不是非黑即白的。可以探讨一个方案在带来好处的同时，可能产生的潜在问题。
  • 提出反驳：在论证自己观点时，可以先承认对立观点有一定道理（Admittedly,…），然后再进行反驳（However,…），这能体现思维的全面性。

第四部分：小作文 (Task 1) 写作攻略

小作文要求在20分钟内写一篇不少于150词的图表描述。核心是客观描述、数据支撑、对比归纳，不要发表个人观点。

通用四段式结构

第一段：引言 (Introduction)

任务：改写题目，说明图表类型、内容和时间。

句型：“The provided [chart type] illustrates/compares/shows [what the chart is about] from [start time] to [end time].”

第二段：概述 (Overview)

任务：总结图表最核心、最明显的特征或总体趋势，不涉及具体数据。

句型：“Overall, it is clear that…” / “The most striking feature is…” / “As can be seen from the chart,…”

第三段：细节描述 1 (Details 1)

任务：选取图表中的一组关键数据进行描述和对比。可以按时间、类别或数值大小分组。

句型：“Regarding [category A], it started at [value] in [year] and then…”

第四段：细节描述 2 (Details 2)

任务：描述另一组关键数据，并尽可能与前一段的数据进行对比。

句型：“In contrast, the figure for [category B] showed a different pattern, …”

7类图表题型详解

1. 表格题 (Table)

核心：数据量大，需要有选择性地描述，突出最大/最小值和对比。

概述：找出横向或纵向对比中数值最大和最小的项。

细节：分行或分列进行描述。例如，先描述第一行的数据特点，再描述第二行，并与第一行对比。

词汇：accounted for (占了), the figure for… was (…的数字是), respectively (分别地)。

2. 饼图题 (Pie Chart)

核心：描述各个部分所占的比例 (proportion/percentage)。

概述：找出占比最大和最小的板块。

细节：将占比相近或相关的项放在一起描述，进行对比。

词汇：constituted the largest proportion at X% (构成了最大比例，为X%), made up the smallest percentage (占比最小), followed by (其次是)。

3. 线图题 (Line Chart)

核心：描述数据随时间变化的趋势 (trend)。

概述：描述总体的上升、下降、波动或平稳趋势。

细节：按时间分段描述，重点描述起点、终点、峰值和谷值，以及显著的转折点。

趋势词汇：

  • 上升：increase, rise, grow, climb (v.) / an increase, a rise (n.)
  • 下降：decrease, fall, drop, decline (v.) / a decrease, a fall (n.)
  • 平稳：remain stable/steady, level off
  • 波动：fluctuate (v.) / a fluctuation (n.)
  • 程度副词：sharply, dramatically, significantly (剧烈地) / steadily, gradually (平稳地) / slightly (轻微地)。

4. 柱图题 (Bar Chart)

核心：对比不同项目/类别的数据。

概述：找出数值最高和最低的柱子。

细节：将相似或有对比价值的柱子分组进行描述。如果带时间轴，则兼具线图特征，需要描述变化。

对比词汇：was significantly higher than (远高于), was the lowest among all (在所有中是最低的), in comparison with (与…相比)。

5. 地图题 (Map)

核心：描述一个地点在两个不同时间点的变化。

概述：总结这个地方发生的总体变化，如变得更现代化、工业化或绿化面积减少。

细节：按方位（东北角 a northeastern corner，中心 in the center）描述具体设施的变化。

变化词汇：

  • 新增：was built/constructed, was added
  • 拆除/取代：was demolished/removed and replaced by…
  • 改造/扩大：was converted into…, was expanded
  • 方位词：to the north of, adjacent to, opposite

6. 混合图 (Mixed Charts)

核心：结合两种图表的信息，并找出它们之间的关联。

概述：分别概括两个图表的主要特征，并点出可能的联系。

细节：一段描述一个图。例如，第一段描述柱图的数据对比，第二段描述线图的趋势，并在结尾处尝试建立联系（如"柱图中显示的A国高消费可能解释了线图中该国高肥胖率的趋势"）。

7. 流程图 (Process Diagram)

核心：按顺序清晰地描述一个过程的各个步骤。

概述：说明这个流程有多少个步骤，以及起点和终点是什么。

细节：使用表示顺序的连接词，一步一步地描述。

顺序词汇：The process begins when…, In the first stage, …, Next/Then/After that, …, Following this, …, The penultimate step is…, Finally/In the final stage, …

句型：多使用被动语态，如 The raw materials are transported to the factory. (原材料被运到工厂)。

小作文高分技巧

1. 数据描述、选择与组织

精确描述比例：

  • X accounted for / comprised / constituted / made up 30% of the total.
  • The proportion of X was 30%.
  • A third of… (三分之一), A quarter of… (四分之一), The vast majority of… (绝大多数)。

精确描述趋势与程度：

  • 波动: fluctuated around…
  • 平稳: remained stable/constant at…, levelled off at…
  • 峰值/谷值: peaked at…, reached a peak of…, hit a low of…
  • 程度: a significant/dramatic/sharp increase, a moderate/steady/gradual fall, a slight/marginal decline。

数据选择：不要罗列所有数据。选择起点、终点、最高点、最低点、交点、转折点以及最大和最小的对比项。

信息组织：

  • 线图：按时间顺序分段，或按线的特征（如一条上升，一条下降）分段。
  • 柱图/饼图：按数值大小（从大到小）或类别（如男性一组，女性一组）分段。

2. 图表间关系分析 (混合图)

任务：找出两个图表之间的内在联系。

方法：先分别描述两个图表的主要特征。然后在第二个细节段或结论段，尝试建立联系。

句型：“The trend shown in the line graph might be explained by the data in the bar chart. For example, the rising unemployment rate (line graph) corresponds with the closure of several major factories (bar chart).”

3. 特殊图表处理

多线图/多柱图：不要试图描述每一条线或每一个柱子。选择最有代表性的2-3个进行详细描述，其他的可以一笔带过（while the figures for A, B, and C remained relatively low.）。

流程图/地图题：

  • 方位词要准：in the northeast, to the west of, was located on the southern bank of the river。
  • 动词要准：was constructed, was demolished, was converted into, was extended。
  • 流程图多用被动语态和顺序词。
