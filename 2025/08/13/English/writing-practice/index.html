大作文

题目（综合类）

Many aspects of the way people dress today are influenced by global fashion trends.

How has global fashion become such a strong influence on people’s lives?

Do you think this is a positive or negative development?

Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience.

Write at least 250 words.

原始文章全文（6分）

It is true that people’s dressing styles are influenced by global fashion trends. There could be several reasons for this, and I consider this to be a negative development.

The main reason is that information is spread quickly through social media and people tend to be influenced by all kinds of pictures and videos that go viral in the Internet. For example, celebrities share their outfit of the day, e.g. OOTD, in tiktok or insgram. However, the beautiful contents are usually edited with filters to flatter themselves and attract more viewers. Some viewers may see the posts by chance and think the dressing style is suitable for them as well. As a result, more and more people are influenced and try getting used to the new fashions. Another reason is that some young people are easily to chase the hits or fashion in the world. They may take the new fashion as advantages over others. This ideology of comparing stimulates the new trend in the youngsters.

In my opinion, I completely disagree with this scenario. First of all, dressing as the fashion trends doesn’t show people’s personalities. Because everyone is unique and different, dressing the same way as others can’t make the real ego of us shining. Secondly, some people pursue the fa0hsion trend not for the style, but for the advantages over others instead. This thought will make these people overlook the real beauty of dressing, causing them to get lost in others’ flattering. Furthermore, the fashion may be forged in quick succession by some companies to trap money out of the costumers. It is probable that these fashion trends are out of date in a few weeks. If we buy clothes as the trends, we will dump the clothes as the trends change.

In conclusion, dressing as the global fashion trends is not sustainable. We should try to flatter ourselves out of our own aesthetics.

逐句修改（8分标准）

Introduction (引言段)

原句：It is true that people’s dressing styles are influenced by global fashion trends. 修改后： It is true that people’s dressing styles are influenced by global fashion trends. → It is undeniable that global fashion trends increasingly dictate how people dress.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：It is true that 是一个比较基础的开头，It is undeniable that (不可否认的是) 语气更强，更具学术性。people's dressing styles (人们的穿衣风格) 表达略显口语化，how people dress (人们如何穿着) 更简洁地道。are influenced by (被…影响) 是被动语态，虽然正确，但 dictate (决定，支配) 这个词更生动、精准地表达了时尚潮流的强大影响力。
  • 结构规范：这是一个清晰的主题句，直接引出文章要讨论的核心现象。

原句：There could be several reasons for this, and I consider this to be a negative development. 修改后： There could be several reasons for this, and I consider this to be a negative development. → While this phenomenon can be attributed to several key factors, I contend that its overall impact is largely negative.

修改说明：

  • 语法清晰性：原句用 and 连接两个独立的想法，结构简单。修改后使用了 While..., I contend that... (尽管…，但我认为…) 的复合句结构，展示了更强的句子掌控能力，也使文章的论点（thesis statement）更加清晰有力。
  • 词汇优化：reasons → key factors (关键因素)；consider → contend (主张，认为)，后者在议论文中更正式；negative development → largely negative impact (很大程度上是负面影响)，表述更精确。
Body Paragraph 1 (主体段一：分析原因)

原句：The main reason is that information is spread quickly through social media and people tend to be influenced by all kinds of pictures and videos that go viral in the Internet. 修改后： The main reason is that information is spread quickly through social media and people tend to be influenced by all kinds of pictures and videos that go viral in the Internet. → A primary driver of this trend is the rapid dissemination of information via social media, where viral images and videos exert considerable influence over public taste.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：The main reason → A primary driver (一个主要驱动力)，表达更高级。information is spread quickly → the rapid dissemination of information (信息的快速传播)，将动词短语名词化，是学术写作的常用技巧。be influenced by → exert considerable influence over (对…施加巨大影响)，变被动为主动，句子更有力。all kinds of → viral 已经包含了这层意思，无需赘述。
  • 逻辑强化：将原句中两个分散的想法（信息传播快 & 人们被影响）整合为一个因果关系更紧密的句子。

原句：For example, celebrities share their outfit of the day, e.g. OOTD, in tiktok or insgram. 修改后： For example, celebrities share their outfit of the day, e.g. OOTD, in tiktok or insgram. → For instance, celebrities and influencers frequently showcase their ‘outfit of the day’ on platforms like TikTok and Instagram.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：e.g. 和 OOTD 过于口语化和缩写，不适合正式写作，可以直接描述。share → showcase (展示)，更正式。增加了 influencers (网红/影响者)，使例子更贴近当前社会现实。
  • 规范性：拼写错误 insgram → Instagram。

原句：However, the beautiful contents are usually edited with filters to flatter themselves and attract more viewers. 修改后： However, the beautiful contents are usually edited with filters to flatter themselves and attract more viewers. → These meticulously curated posts, often enhanced with filters, present an idealized image designed to attract a wider audience.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：beautiful contents → meticulously curated posts (精心策划的帖子)，描述更精准。edited with filters to flatter themselves → enhanced with filters (用滤镜美化) 和 present an idealized image (呈现一种理想化的形象)，表达更客观、学术。attract more viewers → attract a wider audience (吸引更广泛的受众)，用词更地道。

原句：Some viewers may see the posts by chance and think the dressing style is suitable for them as well. As a result, more and more people are influenced and try getting used to the new fashions. 修改后： Some viewers may see the posts by chance and think the dressing style is suitable for them as well. As a result, more and more people are influenced and try getting used to the new fashions. → Consequently, followers may be persuaded to emulate these styles, believing they can replicate the same aspirational look.

修改说明：

  • 逻辑强化：将两个短句合并，逻辑关系更紧密。As a result → Consequently (因此)，衔接更流畅。
  • 词汇优化：viewers → followers (关注者)，在社交媒体语境下更准确。think the dressing style is suitable → be persuaded to emulate these styles (被说服去模仿这些风格)，emulate 是一个非常好的动词。try getting used to → replicate the same aspirational look (复制同样令人向往的造型)，表达更生动、具体。

原句：Another reason is that some young people are easily to chase the hits or fashion in the world. 修改后： Another reason is that some young people are easily to chase the hits or fashion in the world. → Furthermore, there is a pervasive tendency, particularly among the youth, to pursue the latest trends.

修改说明：

  • 语法修正：are easily to chase 是错误的，应为 easily chase 或 find it easy to chase。
  • 词汇优化：Another reason is → Furthermore (此外)，过渡更自然。chase the hits or fashion → pursue the latest trends (追逐最新潮流)，表达更书面化。pervasive tendency (普遍的趋势) 是一个很好的词伙。

原句：They may take the new fashion as advantages over others. This ideology of comparing stimulates the new trend in the youngsters. 修改后： They may take the new fashion as advantages over others. This ideology of comparing stimulates the new trend in the youngsters. → This desire to appear fashionable often stems from a sense of competition, where adopting new styles is perceived as a way to gain social currency over peers, thereby fueling the cycle of trends.

修改说明：

  • 逻辑强化：将两个简单的句子融合成一个复杂的因果解释链。
  • 词汇优化：advantages over others → gain social currency over peers (在同龄人中获得社交资本)，这是一个非常地道且深刻的表达。ideology of comparing → stems from a sense of competition (源于一种竞争感)，解释了背后的心理动机。stimulates the new trend → fueling the cycle of trends (为潮流循环火上浇油)，用词更形象。
Body Paragraph 2 (主体段二：阐述负面影响)

原句：In my opinion, I completely disagree with this scenario. First of all, dressing as the fashion trends doesn’t show people’s personalities. 修改后： In my opinion, I completely disagree with this scenario. First of all, dressing as the fashion trends doesn’t show people’s personalities. → From my perspective, this development is regrettable for several reasons. Firstly, conforming to ubiquitous fashion trends can stifle individuality.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：I completely disagree 略显主观和绝对，this development is regrettable (这种发展令人遗憾) 表达了负面立场但更客观。dressing as the fashion trends → conforming to ubiquitous fashion trends (遵从无处不在的时尚潮流)，ubiquitous (无处不在的) 和 conform to (遵从) 都是高分词汇。doesn't show people's personalities → stifle individuality (扼杀个性)，表达简洁有力。

原句：Because everyone is unique and different, dressing the same way as others can’t make the real ego of us shineing. 修改后： Because everyone is unique and different, dressing the same way as others can’t make the real ego of us shineing. → Each person possesses a unique identity, and adopting a uniform style of dress prevents their true character from being expressed.

修改说明：

  • 语法修正：shineing → shining。real ego of us 表达非常中式，不地道。
  • 词汇优化：everyone is unique and different → Each person possesses a unique identity (每个人都拥有独特的身份)。dressing the same way as others → adopting a uniform style of dress (采用千篇一律的穿着风格)。make the real ego shine → prevents their true character from being expressed (阻碍了他们真实个性的表达)。

原句：Secondly, some people pursue the fa0hsion trend not for the style, but for the advantages over others instead. 修改后： Secondly, some people pursue the fa0hsion trend not for the style, but for the advantages over others instead. → Secondly, the motivation behind following these trends is often misplaced, focusing on social comparison rather than a genuine appreciation for the style itself.

修改说明：

  • 逻辑强化：原句的 not for... but for... instead 有些重复。修改后的句子更清晰地指出了问题核心：动机错位 (misplaced motivation)。
  • 词汇优化：advantages over others → social comparison (社会比较)，更学术。not for the style → rather than a genuine appreciation for the style itself (而非出于对风格本身的真正欣赏)，解释得更充分。

原句：This thought will make these people overlook the real beauty of dressing, causing them to get lost in others’ flattering. 修改后： This thought will make these people overlook the real beauty of dressing, causing them to get lost in others’ flattering. → This mindset detracts from the intrinsic value of personal style and can lead to a superficial pursuit of validation from others.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：thought → mindset (思维模式)。real beauty of dressing → intrinsic value of personal style (个人风格的内在价值)，表达更精确。get lost in others' flattering → a superficial pursuit of validation from others (对他人认可的肤浅追求)，validation (认可) 是一个关键的心理学词汇，用在这里非常贴切。

原句：Furthermore, the fashion may be forged in quick succession by some companies to trap money out of the costumers. 修改后： Furthermore, the fashion may be forged in quick succession by some companies to trap money out of the costumers. → Finally, the very nature of ‘fast fashion’ is problematic, as many trends are deliberately engineered by corporations to have a short lifespan, encouraging constant consumption.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：forged in quick succession → engineered... to have a short lifespan (被设计为拥有短暂的生命周期)。trap money out of the costumers → encouraging constant consumption (鼓励持续消费)。引入了关键术语 ‘fast fashion’ (快时尚)，使论证更具现实意义和深度。costumers → consumers。

原句：It is probable that these fashion trends are out of date in a few weeks. If we buy clothes as the trends, we will dump the clothes as the trends change. 修改后： It is probable that these fashion trends are out of date in a few weeks. If we buy clothes as the trends, we will dump the clothes as the trends change. → This business model not only exploits consumers but also promotes a disposable culture, where garments are discarded as soon as they are deemed unfashionable, leading to significant environmental waste.

修改说明：

  • 逻辑强化：将快时尚的后果提升到了两个层面：剥削消费者 (exploits consumers) 和导致环境问题 (leading to significant environmental waste)。
  • 词汇优化：dump the clothes → promotes a disposable culture (助长了一种“用后即弃”的文化)，discarded (被丢弃)。out of date → deemed unfashionable (被认为不时尚)。整个句子结构更复杂，论证更有力。
Conclusion (结论段)

原句：In conclusion, dressing as the global fashion trends is not sustainable. We should try to flatter ourselves out of our own aesthetics. 修改后： In conclusion, dressing as the global fashion trends is not sustainable. We should try to flatter ourselves out of our own aesthetics. → In conclusion, while the global reach of fashion is driven by digital media and social pressures, its consequences—namely the erosion of individuality and the promotion of a wasteful, consumerist culture—are profoundly negative. Ultimately, developing a personal aesthetic, rather than passively following transient trends, is a far more sustainable and fulfilling approach to style.

修改说明：

  • 结构规范：结论段应该先总结前文论点，再提出建议或展望。修改后的句子首先用 while... 句型总结了潮流的驱动力（对应第一段），然后用 its consequences... are profoundly negative 总结了其负面影响（对应第二段），结构非常完整。
  • 词汇优化：not sustainable → the erosion of individuality (个性的侵蚀) 和 a wasteful, consumerist culture (浪费的消费主义文化)，总结得更具体。flatter ourselves out of our own aesthetics 这种表达不地道，修改为 developing a personal aesthetic (发展个人审美)。transient trends (短暂的潮流) 是对 fashion trends 的一个很好的同义替换。fulfilling approach (更有成就感的方式) 提升了立意。

修改后全文（8分标准）

It is undeniable that global fashion trends increasingly dictate how people dress. While this phenomenon can be attributed to several key factors, I contend that its overall impact is largely negative.

A primary driver of this trend is the rapid dissemination of information via social media, where viral images and videos exert considerable influence over public taste. For instance, celebrities and influencers frequently showcase their ‘outfit of the day’ on platforms like TikTok and Instagram. These meticulously curated posts, often enhanced with filters, present an idealized image designed to attract a wider audience. Consequently, followers may be persuaded to emulate these styles, believing they can replicate the same aspirational look. Furthermore, there is a pervasive tendency, particularly among the youth, to pursue the latest trends. This desire to appear fashionable often stems from a sense of competition, where adopting new styles is perceived as a way to gain social currency over peers, thereby fueling the cycle of trends.

From my perspective, this development is regrettable for several reasons. Firstly, conforming to ubiquitous fashion trends can stifle individuality. Each person possesses a unique identity, and adopting a uniform style of dress prevents their true character from being expressed. Secondly, the motivation behind following these trends is often misplaced, focusing on social comparison rather than a genuine appreciation for the style itself. This mindset detracts from the intrinsic value of personal style and can lead to a superficial pursuit of validation from others. Finally, the very nature of ‘fast fashion’ is problematic, as many trends are deliberately engineered by corporations to have a short lifespan, encouraging constant consumption. This business model not only exploits consumers but also promotes a disposable culture, where garments are discarded as soon as they are deemed unfashionable, leading to significant environmental waste.

In conclusion, while the global reach of fashion is driven by digital media and social pressures, its consequences—namely the erosion of individuality and the promotion of a wasteful, consumerist culture—are profoundly negative. Ultimately, developing a personal aesthetic, rather than passively following transient trends, is a far more sustainable and fulfilling approach to style.

8分作文核心特征总结

  1. 词汇实用性 (Lexical Resource)
    • 主题词伙运用：文章使用了大量与“时尚”、“社会”和“环境”主题高度相关的词伙，如 dictate how people dress (决定穿着方式), rapid dissemination of information (信息快速传播), meticulously curated posts (精心策划的帖子), emulate styles (模仿风格), stifle individuality (扼杀个性), social comparison (社会比较), fast fashion (快时尚), disposable culture (一次性文化), personal aesthetic (个人审美), transient trends (短暂潮流)。
    • 高频词精准度：将 reason 升级为 driver, factor；将 influence 升级为 dictate, exert influence；将 follow 升级为 pursue, emulate, conform to。这些词汇在议论文中更常见且表达更精确。
    • 避免生僻词：所用词汇均为标准学术用语，没有使用过于生僻或华而不实的词语，保证了文章的自然流畅。
  2. 语法清晰性 (Grammatical Range and Accuracy)
    • 句式多样性：文章融合了多种句式。例如，使用了While..., ...开头的让步状语从句，where...引导的定语从句，以及现在分词短语作状语（thereby fueling..., leading to...），展示了娴熟的语法功底。
    • 错误修正：修正了原稿中的语法错误，如 are easily to chase 和 shineing，以及不地道的表达如 real ego of us。
    • 标点规范：全文标点使用准确无误，保证了句子的清晰度。
  3. 论证逻辑性 (Coherence and Cohesion)
    • 原因/影响分层：主体段落的论点组织清晰。第一段从“社交媒体”和“同辈压力”两个方面解释了潮流形成的原因。第二段则从“个人层面（失去个性）”、“社会心理层面（错误动机）”到“产业与环境层面（快时尚的危害）”分层论证其负面影响，逻辑层次感强。
    • 论点递进关系：段落内部的句子之间衔接紧密，层层递进。例如，从“网红发帖”到“粉丝模仿”，再到“背后的竞争心理”，形成了一条完整的逻辑链。
    • 例证有效性：用社交媒体（TikTok, Instagram）作为例子，非常贴切且具有说服力，有效地支撑了论点。
  4. 结构规范性 (Task Response & Cohesion)
    • 四段式框架：文章采用了标准的“引言-论证1-论证2-结论”四段式结构，完全符合雅思大作文的要求。
    • 主旨句位置：引言段末尾有清晰的观点句（thesis statement），每个主体段开头都有明确的主题句（topic sentence），让读者一目了然。
    • 衔接词使用：恰当使用了 For instance, Furthermore, Consequently, Firstly, Secondly, Finally, In conclusion 等衔接词，使得段落和句子之间的过渡自然流畅，逻辑清晰。

题目（利弊比较类）

Some people have decided to reduce the number of times they fly every year or to stop flying altogether. Do you think the environmental benefits of this development outweigh the disadvantages for individuals and businesses? Give reasons for your answer and include any relevant examples from your own knowledge or experience. Write at least 250 words.

原始文章全文

Some people have determined to minimize the number of times they fly or even stop flying at all. While I accept that this development have a positive impact on environment, I contend that it’s more likely to have a negative impact for individuals and businesses.

On the one hand, the airline may cancel some flights because of the decrement of passengers. This could mean that the total gas consumed by airplanes globally is reduced. As a result, the carbon dioxide is less discharged and the climate change is finally alleviated. Furthermore, if these people chose eco-friendly transports such as driving EVs and taking bullet trains which are driven by electricity as well, the carbon dioxide released by cancellation of flights is 100 percent reduced, without any subtraction for that produced by other transports.

However, I would argue that these benefits are outweighted by the drawbacks. First of all, as is known to us, taking a plane is the fatest way of transportation in the world. Giving up on airplanes means that the time spent on the way to the destination mutiplizes, which may bring problems to individuals and businesses. For instance, I live in Guangzhou which is in south of China. If I am informed at 8 a.m. that I should attend a interview in Beijing(in north of China) at 8 p.m. the same day and I should not take a plane, I will definitely miss the interview. Consequently, I may lose a great opportunity. Moreover, if it is a CEO of a company that dosen’t fly to an important meeting, this company could lose a business that can earn a great deal of money. Furthermore, if people replace flying with driving or taking a train, the indivisual average carbon dioxide released may be not less than that of flying.

In conclusion, it seems to me that potential disadvantages of reducing the number of flying times are more prominent than the advantages.

逐句修改（8分标准）

第一段：引言
原句：Some people have determined to minimize the number of times they fly or even stop flying at all.
修改后：
Some people have determined to minimize the number of times they fly or even stop flying at all. → A growing number of people have resolved to curtail their frequency of air travel, or even cease flying altogether.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：determined to minimize → resolved to curtail，curtail（缩减）是更书面、更精准的表达。stop flying at all → cease flying altogether，cease和altogether的搭配更正式。A growing number of people是作文中常用的高级开头。

原句：While I accept that this development have a positive impact on environment, I contend that it’s more likely to have a negative impact for individuals and businesses.
修改后：
While I accept that this development have a positive impact on environment, I contend that it’s more likely to have a negative impact for individuals and businesses. → While this trend undoubtedly has a positive impact on the environment, I contend that the repercussions for individuals and businesses are far more significant.

修改说明：

  • 语法修正：主谓一致错误 development have → trend has。
  • 词汇优化：positive impact on environment → positive impact on the environment（添加定冠词the）；negative impact → repercussions（负面影响/后果），词汇更高级；more likely to have → are far more significant，语气更强，立场更清晰。
  • 逻辑强化：加入undoubtedly（毫无疑问地）承认其优点，更能突显后文转折的力度。

第二段：让步段（环境优势）
原句：On the one hand, the airline may cancel some flights because of the decrement of passengers.
修改后：
On the one hand, the airline may cancel some flights because of the decrement of passengers. → On the one hand, a significant decline in passenger numbers could compel airlines to cancel certain routes.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：decrement of passengers → a significant decline in passenger numbers，表达更自然、流畅；flights → routes（航线），用词更精确；may cancel → could compel ... to cancel，句式更多样，compel（迫使）比may更具逻辑因果性。

原句：This could mean that the total gas consumed by airplanes globally is reduced.
修改后：
This could mean that the total gas consumed by airplanes globally is reduced. → This would logically lead to a reduction in the total aviation fuel consumed globally.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：gas consumed → aviation fuel consumed，aviation fuel（航空燃油）是专业且准确的词伙。
  • 结构规范：This could mean that...is reduced → This would logically lead to a reduction in...，将动词转换为名词，是学术写作的标志，使句子更客观。

原句：As a result, the carbon dioxide is less discharged and the climate change is finally alleviated.
修改后：
As a result, the carbon dioxide is less discharged and the climate change is finally alleviated. → Consequently, fewer carbon emissions would be released into the atmosphere, helping to mitigate the effects of climate change.

修改说明：

  • 词汇优化：carbon dioxide is less discharged → fewer carbon emissions would be released，carbon emissions（碳排放）是核心词伙；alleviated → mitigate the effects of（减缓…的影响），搭配更地道。finally一词在这里不合逻辑，予以删除。
  • 语法清晰性：使用现在分词短语helping to mitigate...作结果状语，使句子结构更紧凑。

原句：Furthermore, if these people chose eco-friendly transports… the carbon dioxide released by cancellation of flights is 100 percent reduced, without any subtraction for that produced by other transports.
修改后：

  • TA (任务回应)：修正了步骤总数（图示为9个环节，包括最终成品），并给出了一个高级的概述，即将流程划分为三个逻辑阶段。这是8分作文“突出核心特征”的体现，而不是简单地罗列步骤。
  • LR (词汇)：引入了高级词汇和词伙，如 comprises nine distinct stages, beginning with... and culminating in..., finished garments, logically grouped, raw material acquisition, fibre processing, textile creation。
  • GRA (语法)：使用了现在分词短语 (beginning with... and culminating in...)，使句子结构更丰富。

第三段：流程细节 1
原句：Looking at the details, firstly, bamboo is planted by farmers in Spring.
修改后：
Looking at the details, firstly, bamboo is planted by farmers in Spring. → The process commences in the spring with the planting of bamboo.

修改说明：

  • CC (连贯衔接)：删除了口语化的引导短语 Looking at the details，使文章更书面化。
  • LR (词汇)：is planted → commences with the planting of。使用了“动词+名词”的结构，比单一的被动动词更显地道和专业。
  • TA (任务回应)：删除了图中未提及的 “farmers”，确保描述的精准性。

原句：And then, when the bamboo is ripen in autumn, farmers will harvest it.
修改后：
And then, when the bamboo is ripen in autumn, farmers will harvest it. → Once mature, the plants are harvested in the autumn.

修改说明：

  • CC (连贯衔接)：用 Once mature, 替换了重复且口语化的 And then，并巧妙地连接了前后步骤的逻辑关系。
  • GRA (语法)：修正了 is ripen → is ripe/mature 的错误；将主动语态 farmers will harvest it 改为更适合流程图描述的被动语态 the plants are harvested，并统一使用一般现在时。

原句：Collected, a pile of bamboo is cut into strips which are crushed later to make liquid pulp.
修改后：
Collected, a pile of bamboo is cut into strips which are crushed later to make liquid pulp. → Following this, the harvested bamboo is cut into strips and subsequently crushed to form a liquid pulp.

修改说明：

  • GRA (语法)：修正了原句 Collected, 引导的独立主格结构的别扭用法，使句子结构更清晰。
  • CC (连贯衔接)：使用了 Following this 和 subsequently 这两个清晰的顺序连接词。
  • LR (词汇)：make → form，用词更精准。

原句：This pulp is subsequently filtered to separate long fibres from liquid.
修改后：
This pulp is subsequently filtered to separate long fibres from liquid. → This pulp then undergoes filtration to separate the long fibres from the liquid.

修改说明：

  • LR (词汇)：is filtered → undergoes filtration。使用了“动词+名词”的专业词伙，是流程图描述中的加分项，展示了更宽的词汇量。

第四段：流程细节 2
原句：However, the filtered fibres are too tough, which should be soften by adding water and amine oxide.
修改后：
However, the filtered fibres are too tough, which should be soften by adding water and amine oxide. → In the next stage, the separated fibres are softened in a solution of water and amine oxide.

修改说明：

  • CC (连贯衔接)：However (表转折) 使用错误，应为顺序关系，故改为 In the next stage。
  • GRA (语法)：修正了 should be soften → are softened 的情态动词和被动语态错误。
  • LR (词汇)：by adding → in a solution of，用词更科学、精准。

原句：And then, it’s time to spin to make yarn.
修改后：
And then, it’s time to spin to make yarn. → These softened fibres are then spun into yarn.

修改说明：

  • GRA (语法)：将口语化的 it's time to... 结构改为正式的被动语态 are then spun into，符合学术写作规范。
  • LR (词汇)：spin into yarn 是固定搭配，表达简洁有力。

原句：Furthermore, the yarn need to be weaved together to make fabric wich are finally used to be manufactured in the factory to make a clothes such as T-shirts and stockings.
修改后：
Furthermore, the yarn need to be weaved together to make fabric wich are finally used to be manufactured in the factory to make a clothes such as T-shirts and stockings. → Subsequently, this yarn is woven into fabric. Finally, this fabric is used to manufacture finished garments, such as T-shirts and socks.

修改说明：

  • GRA (语法)：将一个冗长且充满语法错误（need主谓不一致, weaved过去分词错误, wich拼写错误, a clothes名词单复数错误）的句子拆分为两个清晰、准确的短句。
  • CC (连贯衔接)：Furthermore (表递进) 使用不当，改为表示顺序的 Subsequently 和表示结尾的 Finally。
  • LR (词汇)：make a clothes → manufacture finished garments，用词高级且准确。

修改后全文（8分标准）

The flow chart illustrates the process by which bamboo fabric is manufactured.

Overall, the manufacturing process comprises nine distinct stages, beginning with the cultivation of bamboo and culminating in the production of finished garments. These stages can be logically grouped into three main phases: raw material acquisition, fibre processing, and textile creation.

The process commences in the spring with the planting of bamboo. Once mature, the plants are harvested in the autumn. Following this, the harvested bamboo is cut into strips and subsequently crushed to form a liquid pulp. This pulp then undergoes filtration to separate the long fibres from the liquid.

In the next stage, the separated fibres are softened in a solution of water and amine oxide. These softened fibres are then spun into yarn. Subsequently, this yarn is woven into fabric. Finally, this fabric is used to manufacture finished garments, such as T-shirts and socks.
(168 words)

8分小作文核心特征总结

  1. 任务回应（TA）：概述段落（第二段）不再是简单地数步骤，而是对整个流程进行了高度概括和逻辑分层（分为三大阶段），精准地把握并报告了图表的核心特征。
  2. 词汇（LR）：大量使用流程图专属的高级词伙（如 commences with, undergoes filtration, culminating in），并用精准的动词和名词（如 comprises, cultivation, garments）替换了原文中重复、简单的词汇（如 make, steps）。
  3. 语法（GRA）：通篇使用一般现在时被动语态（are harvested, is cut, are softened），这是描述客观流程的黄金法则。同时，句式灵活多变，融合了关系从句（by which...）、分词短语（beginning with...）等复杂结构，且无语法错误。
  4. 连贯衔接（CC）：逻辑连接词使用精准且多样（Overall, Following this, subsequently, then, In the next stage, Finally），避免了原文中And then的重复和However/Furthermore的误用，使得整个流程的描述如行云流水，逻辑清晰。文章结构采用标准的“总-分”结构，条理分明。

题型二：表格类

作文题目

The first table below shows changes in the total population of New York City from 1800 to 2000. The second and third tables show changes in the population of the five districts of the city (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Bronx, Queens, Staten Island) over the same period.

Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant.

Write at least 150 words.

原始文章全文(6.0)

These three tables illustrate the changes of population of New York City and its five districts of the city from 1800 to 2000.

The totoal population of New York City rocketed over the past 200 years. While Manhattan accounted for main proportion in 1800 but dropped to almost a quarter in 2000, Other districts is the opposite.

Looking at the details, the total population of New York City was merely around 80,000 in 1800, then prolifarated to almost 3.5 million in 1900, which is close to half of the population in 2000. 1800, three quarters of people lived in Manhattan and a quarter chose to stay in other four districts( Brooklyn, Bronx, Queens, Staten Island).

However, the proportion changed rapidly in later years. In 1900, the proportion of the population of Manhattan and other districts was 54 percent and 46 percent respectively. In 2000, most people dwelled in other districts with the proportion of 81 percent which three times bigger than the figure of 19 percent in Manhattan.

逐句修改（8分标准：TA/CC/LR/GRA）

第一段：引言
原句：These three tables illustrate the changes of population of New York City and its five districts of the city from 1800 to 2000.
修改后：
These three tables illustrate the changes of population of New York City and its five districts of the city from 1800 to 2000. → The three tables present data on the population dynamics of New York City as a whole and the distribution of its residents between Manhattan and the other four districts from 1800 to 2000.

修改说明：

  • 词汇（LR）：illustrate the changes of population → present data on the population dynamics，使用“名词+of+名词”的结构，dynamics（动态变化）比changes更具学术性。its five districts → distribution of its residents between Manhattan and the other four districts，更精确地概括了后两个表格的内容。
  • 语法（GRA）：避免了原文中of the city的重复。

第二段：概述
原句：The totoal population of New York City rocketed over the past 200 years. While Manhattan accounted for main proportion in 1800 but dropped to almost a quarter in 2000, Other districts is the opposite.
修改后：
[原句全文] → Overall, New York City experienced exponential population growth over the two centuries. A more significant trend was the dramatic demographic shift, where Manhattan’s population dominance in 1800 was completely reversed in favour of the other districts by 2000.

修改说明：

  • 任务回应（TA）：清晰地概括出两大核心特征：1. 城市总人口的巨幅增长；2. 曼哈顿与其他区人口比例的戏剧性反转。这是8分作文必须具备的宏观总结能力。
  • 词汇（LR）：rocketed → experienced exponential growth（经历了指数级增长）；main proportion → population dominance（人口主导地位）；is the opposite → was completely reversed in favour of（完全反转， لصالح…）。
  • 连贯衔接（CC）：使用Overall引导概述段。用A more significant trend was...强调第二个核心特征，逻辑层次更清晰。
  • 语法（GRA）：修正了原句的拼写错误（totoal）和主谓不一致错误（districts is）。

第三段：细节1
原句：Looking at the details, the total population of New York City was merely around 80,000 in 1800, then prolifarated to almost 3.5 million in 1900, which is close to half of the population in 2000.
修改后：
[原句全文] → Specifically, the total population of the city surged from just 79,216 in 1800 to over 3.4 million in 1900, before more than doubling to reach approximately 8 million by the year 2000.

修改说明：

  • 词汇（LR）：merely around 80,000 → just 79,216（使用精确数据）；prolifarated（拼写错误且用法生僻） → surged（飙升）；close to half of the population in 2000 → before more than doubling to reach approximately 8 million，用动态的描述（翻倍多）代替了静态的比较，语言更生动。
  • 连贯衔接（CC）：用Specifically替代口语化的Looking at the details。

原句：1800, three quarters of people lived in Manhattan and a quarter chose to stay in other four districts( Brooklyn, Bronx, Queens, Staten Island).
修改后：
[原句全文] → In 1800, the population was heavily concentrated in Manhattan, which was home to 76% of the city’s inhabitants, while the remaining 24% resided in the other districts.

修改说明：

  • 词汇（LR）：lived in → was heavily concentrated in / was home to / resided in，词汇多样化。three quarters → 76%，使用精确数据。
  • 语法（GRA）：使用which引导的非限定性定语从句和while引导的并列句，句式结构比原句的两个简单句并列更复杂、流畅。

第四段：细节2
原句：However, the proportion changed rapidly in later years. In 1900, the proportion of the population of Manhattan and other districts was 54 percent and 46 percent respectively.
修改后：
[原句全文] → This demographic distribution underwent a significant transformation over the following century. By 1900, Manhattan’s share of the population had fallen to 54%, with the other districts’ proportion rising to 46%.

修改说明：

  • 词汇（LR）：the proportion changed rapidly → This demographic distribution underwent a significant transformation（这种人口分布经历了巨大转变），表达非常地道且高级。proportion of the population → share of the population，同义替换。
  • 连贯衔接（CC）：用This demographic distribution指代上文内容，衔接自然。

原句：In 2000, most people dwelled in other districts with the proportion of 81 percent which three times bigger than the figure of 19 percent in Manhattan.
修改后：
[原句全文] → The trend culminated in 2000, when the balance had completely inverted: the outer districts accommodated a commanding 81% of the population, a figure that was more than four times higher than Manhattan’s 19%.

修改说明：

  • 词汇（LR）：The trend culminated in 2000（这一趋势在2000年达到顶峰）是非常高级的表达。most people dwelled in → accommodated a commanding 81%（容纳了占绝对优势的81%）。
  • 任务回应（TA）：进行了精准的比较。three times bigger → more than four times higher (81% / 19% ≈ 4.26)，计算和比较都更准确。
  • 语法（GRA）：修正了原句which three times bigger的语法错误。使用了冒号来引出具体的反转数据，使句子结构清晰有力。

修改后全文（8分标准）

The three tables present data on the population dynamics of New York City as a whole and the distribution of its residents between Manhattan and the other four districts from 1800 to 2000.

Overall, New York City experienced exponential population growth over the two centuries. A more significant trend was the dramatic demographic shift, where Manhattan’s population dominance in 1800 was completely reversed in favour of the other districts by 2000.

Specifically, the total population of the city surged from just 79,216 in 1800 to over 3.4 million in 1900, before more than doubling to reach approximately 8 million by the year 2000. In 1800, the population was heavily concentrated in Manhattan, which was home to 76% of the city’s inhabitants, while the remaining 24% resided in the other districts.

This demographic distribution underwent a significant transformation over the following century. By 1900, Manhattan’s share of the population had fallen to 54%, with the other districts’ proportion rising to 46%. The trend culminated in 2000, when the balance had completely inverted: the outer districts accommodated a commanding 81% of the population, a figure that was more than four times higher than Manhattan’s 19%.
(188 words)

8分小作文核心特征总结

  1. 任务回应（TA）：精准地识别并报告了图表的所有核心特征。概述段落高度概括了两个最关键的趋势（总人口剧增和各区人口比例反转）。细节段落不仅引用了精确数据，还进行了深刻且准确的对比（如“more than four times higher”），完美回应了“make comparisons where relevant”的要求。
  2. 词汇（LR）：通篇使用了丰富、精准且地道的数据/趋势描述专属词伙。例如，用population dynamics, exponential growth, demographic shift, heavily concentrated, underwent a significant transformation, culminated in等高级表达，极大地提升了文章的学术性和专业度，避免了词汇重复。
  3. 语法（GRA）：时态统一（描述过去数据全部使用一般过去时或过去完成时），且语法零错误。句式结构复杂多样，娴熟地运用了非限定性定语从句（..., which was home to...）、while并列句、分词短语（..., before more than doubling...）以及冒号等，展示了对复杂句法的掌控力。
  4. 连贯衔接（CC）：文章结构逻辑清晰，采用“总-分”结构。段落和句子之间通过Overall, Specifically, This demographic distribution, By 1900等逻辑连接词和指代关系紧密串联，信息呈报由宏观到微观，时间线清晰，过渡自然流畅。
